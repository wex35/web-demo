Education

At Grant Hope Ministries, we are committed to empowering children through education by providing school fees, uniforms, supplies, tutoring, and scholarships. We ensure that every child in our care has the opportunity to attend school, gain essential life skills, and build a brighter future. Through your support, we continue to break the cycle of poverty and uplift the next generation in Uganda.
